$SEED TOKENOMICS

This is the official whitepaper for the $SEED token sale. This document outlines the distribution schedule, allocation details, and use of the token proceeds. $SEED is the very first Digital Asset Reserve Token on the Zilliqa blockchain.

Introduction/Overview

$SEED  is a groundbreaking  token introduced on the Zilliqa blockchain, marking the inception of the first-ever digital reserve asset token. The fundamental purpose of $SEED  lies in its unique economic model designed to generate sustainable value for its holders. Through the proceeds from token sales, $SEED  aims to strategically invest in profit-generating protocols. The returns from these investments will be utilized to execute a systematic buyback and burn mechanism, effectively reducing the circulating supply of $SEED  and enhancing its market value.

Token Allocation

Token Allocation Summary for $SEED :

 

1. Advisor (2%):

   - Allocation for advisors who contribute valuable insights and guidance to the $SEED project.

 

2. Private Sale (5%):

   - Reserved for early supporters and private investors who play a crucial role in the initial       development and success of $SEED .

 

3. Public Round (30%):

   - Open to the public, allowing a broad community to participate in the $SEED  project during the public token sale.

 

4. Liquidity (15%):

   - Dedicated to ensuring liquidity on the PlunderSwap DEX, promoting a vibrant and active trading environment for $SEED .

 

5. Marketing (3%):

   - Allocated for strategic marketing initiatives to raise awareness and promote the adoption of $SEED  within the Zilliqa community.

 

6. Team (15%):

   - Reserved for the core team members involved in the development, maintenance, and growth of the $SEED  ecosystem.

 

7. Ecosystem (20%):

   - Invested in various profit-generating protocols, forming the backbone of $SEED 's digital reserve asset concept.

 

8. Staker Rewards (10%):

   - Incentives for the community to hold and stake the $SEED  token, contributing to the stability and longevity of the ecosystem.

 

TOTAL (100%):

   - Represents the comprehensive token allocation ensuring a balanced distribution that aligns with the long-term vision of $SEED . This structure aims to foster a robust and sustainable ecosystem, rewarding all key stakeholders and encouraging active participation within the $SEED  community.

Token Distribution Schedule

The total supply of $SEED is set at 10,000,000 tokens. The Token Generation Event (TGE) will take place on Day 1, with subsequent distributions occurring at predetermined timeframes (monthly and quarterly) over a 57-month period.

Long term Objective

The long-term objective of $SEED  is to establish itself as a robust digital reserve asset, fostering a self-sustaining ecosystem on the Zilliqa blockchain. By consistently reinvesting in proven, revenue generating protocols, $SEED  aims to not only increase its intrinsic value but also to contribute to the broader development of decentralized finance. $SEED is committed to  incentivizing its community through various mechanisms. Rewards for token holders providing liquidity on the PlunderSwap DEX, along with additional incentives for those staking their $SEED  tokens on the PlunderStake SSN, are integral to fostering a committed and engaged community. Through these initiatives, $SEED  strives to create a resilient financial infrastructure, encouraging long-term holding and active participation, thereby solidifying its position as a key protocol in the evolving Zilliqa ecosystem.
